Helsinki

Helsinki is a charming seaside city with a unique twist. Located on the shore of the Gulf of Finland, Helsinki is a place where urban culture meets coastal nature.

Lapland

Lapland is the northern-most region of Finland and an active outdoor destination that's known for its incredible, year-round light phenomena, vast arctic nature, and Santa Claus.

Tampere

Tampere is a unique combination of Lakeland charm and the urban Nordic city. Many public saunas, cultural events, laid-back locals, and the old red-brick industrial buildings next to the Tammerkoski River.

Turku

Turku, Finland's oldest city, is a great destination for history buffs, foodies, and those looking for a laidback city break in a charming Nordic town.
